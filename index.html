About the Course

This 8-week live cohort course, "Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity," focuses on using no-code AI tools to automate tasks. The curriculum is designed to equip professionals with the skills to integrate AI into their workflows, from generating documents and presentations to performing complex data analysis and building custom chatbots.

Module 1: Websites, Documents & Presentations

Learn how LLMs work, generate text, create no-code project websites, and design presentations with AI assistants.

Module 2: AI-Automated Data Analysis

Master AI tools for data analysis, file manipulation, and creating automated reports and visualizations.

Module 3: GenAI for Research & Text Analysis

Explore AI techniques for literature search, text analysis, and knowledge synthesis from large text collections.

Module 4: Custom GPTs and Chatbots

Build custom chatbots for research assistance and knowledge management using advanced LLM architecture.

Module 5: AI for Task Automation

Automate repetitive research tasks, set up email automations, and design AI agents for complex workflows.

Module 6: GenAI for Communication & Outreach

Develop AI-driven approaches for research dissemination, including summaries, figures, and video abstracts.
