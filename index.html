About the Course

An 8-week guided course by The GRAPH Courses.

Course Overview

"Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity" is a hands-on course focused on using no-code AI tools to automate tasks. It covers everything from understanding Large Language Models (LLMs) to building custom GPTs, analyzing data, and creating AI-driven communication materials.

Key Features

  • Weekly live workshops and help sessions.
  • Personalized feedback on assessments.
  • Private community for collaboration.
  • Capstone project coaching and showcase.
  • Focus on practical, real-world application.
